Edukarsa is a dedicated learning platform designed to help students prepare for LPDP ([Lembaga Pengelola Dana Pendidikan](https://lpdp.kemenkeu.go.id/)) scholarships. The website offers comprehensive resources, practice materials, and guidance to support users in achieving their scholarship goals effectively.

## Main Features

- **Responsive Design**: Ensures seamless user experience across devices, including desktops, tablets, and smartphones.

- **Mock Tests and Simulations**: Includes realistic practice tests and application simulations to help users gain confidence and prepare effectively for the LPDP selection process.

## Tech Stacks

Inertia, React, Laravel, Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui.
